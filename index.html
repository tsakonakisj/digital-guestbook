Access Instructions & Parking

Getting Here

Exit Tinos Main Port and take the uphill main road towards the inland villages. Google Maps. Follow signs for Triantaros → Dyo Choria → Arnados → Steni (the "old" scenic road to the villages).

Parking Information

Closed free parking inside the area of the house.

House Rules

  • Respect the neighborhood. Keep noise low, especially midday and at night.
  • No parties or events.
  • Only registered guests may stay.
  • Pets only upon request.
  • Water is precious on the island. Take short showers.
  • Plumbing is sensitive. Use the bathroom bin for paper.
  • Tinos can be windy. Secure shutters/umbrellas and close balcony doors.

Κανόνες Σπιτιού

  • Σεβαστείτε τη γειτονιά. Κρατήστε χαμηλά τον θόρυβο, ιδιαίτερα το μεσημέρι και το βράδυ.
  • Δεν επιτρέπονται πάρτι ή εκδηλώσεις.
  • Μόνο οι δηλωμένοι επισκέπτες μπορούν να διαμείνουν.
  • Κατοικίδια μόνο κατόπιν αιτήματος.
  • Το νερό είναι πολύτιμο στο νησί. Κάντε σύντομα ντους.
  • Η αποχέτευση είναι ευαίσθητη. Χρησιμοποιείτε τον κάδο του μπάνιου για το χαρτί.
  • Στην Τήνο φυσάει συχνά. Ασφαλίστε τα παντζούρια/ομπρέλες και κλείστε τις μπαλκονόπορτες.

Area Guide

Coffee & Breakfast

Amiseli

Amiseli is an all-day café-restaurant on Steni's main square, under the plane tree. It opens for coffee and breakfast, then serves Tinian creative dishes and meze, with a solid list of wines and cocktails. Relaxed setting right in the heart of the village. (5min walk)

Apodrasi

Apodrasi is a café-bar and tavern in the center of Steni. It serves breakfast and coffee in the morning, handmade sweets/crepes during the day, and drinks or cocktails in the evening—often with live-music nights. (3min walk)

Restaurants

Douar-Platanos

Douar (Taverna Platanos) is a classic, family-run meat taverna on Steni's square, right under the big plane tree. It's known for excellent charcoal-grilled meats and produce sourced from Tinos—simple, traditional food done right. A long-time village favorite with a warm, old-school vibe. (3min walk)

Vaptistis winery

Vaptistis Winery is a boutique, family-run winery in Steni village, Tinos. They offer tours and tastings with local snacks, showcasing Tinos grapes (e.g., Assyrtiko and a rosé from local varieties). You can book online. (2min walk)

Beaches

Santa Margarita

Santa Margarita Beach is a quiet, unorganized sandy cove on the east side of Tinos, about 15–17 km from town and ~4.5 km past Steni. There are no facilities—bring water and snacks. (14min drive)

Livada Beach

Livada Beach is a remote, unorganized cove on Tinos' north side, reached via a final stretch of dirt road past Steni/Myrsini. It's famous for its wind- and wave-sculpted rocks and a small stream/wetland at one end—wild scenery, great for photos. (15min drive)

One-Day Itinerary

Plan A (with strong meltemi / north wind)

  • 09:00 | Depart from Steni.
  • 10:00 | South/East beaches (more sheltered): Agios Ioannis Porto or Agios Fokas (long sandy beaches), or Pachia Ammos (short walk over a sand dune).
  • 13:30 | Lunch in Tinos Town or near the beach.
  • 16:00 | Volax quick stop for the rock landscape.
  • 18:00 | Sunset in Isternia or Kardiani.
  • 20:30 | Return to Steni.

Checkout Checklist

Before you leave, please help us prepare for the next guests:

  • Take all personal items (chargers, passports, clothes, beach gear).
  • Check drawers, closets, bathroom shelves, under beds/sofas.
  • Empty the fridge/freezer of opened food you won't take.
  • Switch off lights and appliances (kettle, coffee maker, stove/oven).
  • Close and latch all windows, shutters, and balcony doors (Tinos is windy).

Checklist Αναχώρησης

Πριν φύγετε, παρακαλώ βοηθήστε μας να προετοιμαστούμε για τους επόμενους επισκέπτες:

  • Πάρτε όλα τα προσωπικά σας αντικείμενα (φορτιστές, διαβατήρια, ρούχα, είδη παραλίας).
